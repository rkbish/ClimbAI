    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: March 2025. We believe privacy is a right, not a feature.

    What Data We Collect

    Account information: Name, grade, school, city — used to personalise your report.

    Assessment responses: Your 60 psychometric answers are processed to generate your RIASEC profile. We store these to generate your report.

    AI Mentor conversations: Processed by Anthropic's Claude API. We do not store conversation history beyond your session.

    Usage data: Standard analytics (pages viewed, time, device type). No PII linked to analytics.

    How We Use Your Data

    • To generate your personalised CareerDNA Report
    • To power the AI Mentor with context about your profile
    • To improve our assessment accuracy
    • We do NOT sell your data to third parties, ever.
    • We do NOT use your data for advertising.

    Data Storage & Security

    All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Stored in India in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. Retained for 12 months — request deletion at [email protected].
